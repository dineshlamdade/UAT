Password Policy
  1. Password should have minimum length of 8 characters & maximum length of 20 characters.
  2. Password should be alphanumeric having minimum one uppercase (A-Z), one lowercase (a-z), one numeral (0-9) and one special character ( - , _, &, $, #,@, /,%,!,(,),<,>).
  3. Not to use string of three or more consecutive character in password (aaa).
  4. Not to use Firstname and Lastname in the password.
  5. Password shall expire on 30th day from date of password change.
  6. Three unsuccessful login attempts would lock the account, which can be unlocked by Admin only.
  7. In case you forget the password or your password has expired click on forgot password on the login page.
  8. Change password link sent through email shall become invalid after 7 days.
